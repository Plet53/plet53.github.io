Hex Maker Tactics

PROTOTYPE

Taking Tactics RPGs where they've never gone before: To the Hex Grid!

Hex Grids and their adjacency rules have done a lot for TTRPGs and the feel therein, with AoE rules and movement. Let's take those to the world of the Tactics RPG and see how it goes.

The goal is to have a good editor UI and be able to make and release games a la RPGMaker. Godot and its scripting engine will likely make this easy to accomplish.
